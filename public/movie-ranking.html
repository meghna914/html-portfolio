The Best Dramas according to me

My top 4 dramas of all time

Hometown cha-cha-cha

I really loved the simple concept of it. It beautifully portrayed a subtle yet a beautifull love story in a village backdrop aesthetic.

Crash Landing On You

The North-South korea concept similarto India pakistan concept made it the the most enjoyable drama of all time. The friends of the male lead became the cherry on top. I really loved the over the top extra drama

Squid Game

The most adrenaline rushed drama ever. This kept me on my edge all the time.

Pyramid Game

Most recent yet the most intriguing. No stretched scenes and keeps you engaged throughout its runtime. Worth the 10 hours. Well written characters and an amazing plot.
